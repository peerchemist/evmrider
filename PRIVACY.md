# Privacy and Data Protection

## Summary

The evmrider app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the author or any backend service.
There is no analytics or advertising software in the app, and no telemetry is collected.
The author has no connection to the deployed app and receives no data from it.

## Details

- No user accounts, logins, or identifiers are used.
- No analytics, advertising SDKs, or telemetry frameworks are included.
- No data is sent to any service operated by the author.
- Any network requests are made directly from the app to the user-defined RPC endpoint. The author does not receive or process that data.
